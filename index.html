PregnancyApp

Your trusted companion throughout pregnancy

PregnancyApp helps expectant mothers track their pregnancy journey week by week, monitor baby's development, and stay organized with essential tools — all in one simple, bilingual app.

Features

  • Week-by-Week Tracking — Follow your baby's development and your body's changes with detailed weekly information
  • Kick Counter — Monitor your baby's movements with an easy-to-use kick tracking tool
  • Weight Tracker — Log and visualize your weight progress throughout pregnancy
  • Reminders — Set customizable reminders for appointments, medications, and important milestones
  • Bilingual Support — Full experience in English and Spanish (Español)
  • Offline Access — Core features work without an internet connection

Privacy First

Your pregnancy data stays on your device. We never transmit your personal health information. Learn more in our Privacy Policy.

Legal

Please review our Terms of Use before using the app. PregnancyApp is an informational tool and is not a medical device. Always consult your healthcare provider.
