2. Information We Collect

2.1 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

PregnancyApp stores the following information locally on your device using SQLite encrypted database. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server:

  • Last menstrual period (LMP) and estimated due date
  • Weight records and tracking history
  • Kick counter sessions and counts
  • Pregnancy reminders and notifications
  • Application preferences (language, settings)
  • Weekly pregnancy notes and symptoms

2.2 Sensitive Health Data

Important: PregnancyApp stores reproductive health information locally on your device. This data is considered sensitive under applicable data protection laws. We do not collect, transmit, or have access to this information. It remains solely on your device.

2.3 Automatically Collected Data (Analytics)

When you use PregnancyApp, our third-party analytics providers may automatically collect:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • App usage events (screens visited, features used)
  • Session duration and frequency
  • Crash and error reports
  • Language and region settings

This data is anonymous and aggregated — it cannot be used to identify you personally.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected anonymous data for the following purposes:

  • App improvement: Understand how features are used to enhance the user experience
  • Bug fixes: Identify and resolve technical issues
  • Analytics: Generate aggregate statistics about app usage
  • Advertising: Serve relevant ads in the free version (opt-out available)

We never sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

5. Advertising

The free version of PregnancyApp may display advertisements powered by Google AdMob. AdMob may use advertising identifiers and device information to serve personalized ads.

You can opt out of personalized advertising:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID or opt out of personalization
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"

Please note that opting out does not remove ads — it only stops personalized ad targeting.

6. Data Retention

AdMob data Retained according to Google's advertising policies

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to the processing of your personal data
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances

Since PregnancyApp stores data locally on your device, you can exercise most of these rights directly through the app's Settings screen or by uninstalling the application.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • All pregnancy data is stored locally using SQLite encrypted databases
  • No personal health data is transmitted over the internet
  • Communications with third-party services use encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS)
  • We do not maintain servers that store user data

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

PregnancyApp is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information promptly. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

10. Notifications and Permissions

PregnancyApp may request the following device permissions:

  • Notifications: Used exclusively for pregnancy-related reminders (appointments, kick counting, milestones). You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.
  • Storage: Used to store pregnancy data locally on your device.

These permissions are not used for any purpose other than providing the app's core functionality.
